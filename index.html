Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I determine the right dumpster size?

Choosing the right size depends on your debris type and volume. A 10 yard dumpster is best for heavy materials like dirt, while 20 yard and 30 yard dumpsters suit remodeling projects. We can help you estimate based on your description.

2. Can I throw tires in the dumpster?

No, tires are generally prohibited in a standard roll off dumpster rental because they damage landfill equipment and trap gases. You must dispose of them at specialized facilities or pay a separate surcharge per tire.

3. What is the rental duration?

Our standard rental period is usually 7 to 10 days. However, if you are tackling a longer project, we can arrange extended terms for your waste management dumpster rental for a daily fee

4. Do you recycle the waste?

We strive to be environmentally responsible. Whenever possible, we haul debris to transfer stations that sort and recycle materials like wood, metal, and concrete, rather than taking everything from your garbage rental straight to the landfill.

5. How much space is needed for delivery?

The delivery truck needs significant clearance—about 60 feet of straight space and 23 feet of vertical clearance. Please ensure no low-hanging wires or tree branches obstruct the path for your dumpster delivery.
